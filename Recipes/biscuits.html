Biscuits

4 c all-purpose flour mix cut together gradually add and mix cut out bake at 350°F
5 T baking powder
1 t salt
4 T sugar
½ c shortening  
enough milk to make firm, yet pliable  
  1. Preheat oven to 350 °F
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Cut in shortening.
  4. Gradually add milk until the whole thing becomes firm, yet pliable.
  5. Roll out and cut into biscuit shapes
  6. Bake until done.

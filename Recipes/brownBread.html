Brown Bread (with bread machine assist)

3 T margarine   add to bucket make a well run dough cycle put in greased pan(s) and shape cover and let rise 1 hour bake at 325°F for 45 minutes
1¼ c water
1/3 c molasses
3½ c flour stir together in separate bowl
¾ c rolled oats
1 t salt
2 t yeast   add to bucket and cover
½ t sugar   add to bucket
  1. Put margarine, water, and molasses in the bread machine bucket
  2. Stir flour, rolled oats, and salt together in a bowl
  3. Put dry ingredients in bread machine bucket
  4. Make a well in the top of the dry ingredients
  5. Put the yeast in the well and cover with the flour
  6. Sprinkle the sugar on top
  7. Run the bread machine dough cycle
  8. Put the dough in greased bread pan (or pans)
  9. Cover and let rise for one hour
  10. Bake at 325°F for 45 minutes

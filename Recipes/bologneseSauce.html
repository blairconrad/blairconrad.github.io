Bolognese Sauce

½ c olive oil brown over medium heat cook for 30 minutes
2 t finely chopped garlic
½ # beef, diced
½ # pork, diced
1 T dried oregano
1 T finely chopped fresh parsely  
¼ t red pepper flakes
2 c chopped canned plum tomatoes
salt
pepper
  1. Over medium heat, mix oil, garlic, beef, pork, and oregano until brown
  2. Add remaining ingredients and cook for 30 minutes

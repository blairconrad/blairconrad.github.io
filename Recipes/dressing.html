Dressing

4-5 potatoes boil mash mix bake at 325-350°F for a while
1 onion fry up  
margarine
summer savoury  
up to 1 c bread crumbs
  1. Boil 4-5 potatoes
  2. Fry an onion in some margarine
  3. Mash potatoes
  4. onion, bread crumbs, and summer savour together
  5. Bake for about an hour at 325-350°F

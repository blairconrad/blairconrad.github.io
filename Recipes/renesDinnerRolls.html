Rene's Dinner Rolls

1 c warm water add to warmed bread machine bucket, in order run bread machine on "dough" cycle roll out shape into 2-ounce balls put in pan proof 45 minutes bake at 350°F for 15–20 minutes flip out and tops brush with butter
1 egg
generous 2 T margarine
¼ c sugar
1 t salt
3¼ c flour
3 t yeast
  1. Pour warm water in bread maker bucket to preheat. Dump out water
  2. Put everything in bread maker bucket, in the order listed
  3. Have the bread maker make dough
  4. Roll out into a thick rope (2″ wide)
  5. Using buttered hands and knife, cut off approximately 2-ounce chunks, shape them into balls, and place in pan
  6. Proof for 45 minutes
  7. Bake at 350°F for 15–20 minutes, checking bottom—when it's a little brown, it's done
  8. Remove to a wire rack and brush with butter, for a nice shine

Yield: ~16 rolls

<ol>
  <li>Thaw for 24 hours.</li>
  <li>Bring pot of water to boil. Remove from heat.</li>
  <li>Immerse duck bag for 15 minutes.</li>
  <li>Broil until crispy, about 10 minutes or so.</li>
</ol>

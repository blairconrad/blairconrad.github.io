Decadent Double Chocolate Chip Cookies

1⅔ c flour (205 g) combine beat stir bake 9–11 minutes at 375°F cool first on sheets, then on racks
⅓ c cocoa (40 g)
1 t baking soda
½ t salt
1 c butter (227 g) cream
¾ c lightly packed brown sugar (150 g)
½ c granulated sugar (94 g)
1 egg
1 t vanilla
297 g dark chocolate chips  
1¼ c (~130 g) coarsely chopped pecans (optional)
  • Whisk together flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  • Cream butter and sugars with egg and vanilla.
  • Add flour mixture to butter mixture and beat until combined.
  • Stir in chocolate chips and pecans.
  • Drop onto cookie sheets. I use a #40 disher.
  • Bake at 375°F for 9–11 minutes or until set.
  • Cool for 5 minutes on sheets, then transfer to racks and cool completely.

    Yield: 3 dozen

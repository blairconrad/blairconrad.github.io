Grape-Nuts Bread

1⅔ c water Add to bread pan in order Bake in bread maker at medium crust setting
4 T (60 g) vegetable oil
2 T (24 g) sugar
1 t (7.2 g) salt
1 c (116 g) Grape-Nuts cereal
4 c (516 g) bread flour
2 t (9.3 g) active dry yeast
1 T (10 g) gluten (optional)

Makes 1 loaf

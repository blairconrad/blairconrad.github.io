Blind-Baking a Pie Crust

  1. Prick crust all over with a fork
  2. Freeze pan until solid, about 20 minutes
  3. Lightly coat aluminum foil with butter or nonstick spray and press tightly to crust
  4. Bake 20 minutes at 400°F
  5. Carefully remove foil. Patch with dough scraps, if necessary
  6. Bake 10â€“12 minutes until light to deep gold

Magic Cookie Bars

¼ c butter, melted combine pour layer press bake 25–30 minutes at 350° loosen cool in pan
1½ c graham cracker crumbs
1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk  
1⅓ c (8 oz) semi-sweet chocolate chips, or mixture of chocolate and butterscotch  
1 c chopped nuts
1⅓ c flaked coconut
  1. Combine graham cracker crumbs and butter in a small bowl.
  2. Press crumb mixture into greased 9″×13″ baking pan.
  3. Pour sweetened condensed milk over crumbs.
  4. Layer chocolate chips, coconut, and nuts over milk.
  5. Press chunks down with a fork.
  6. Bake 25–30 minutes at 350° or until lightly browned.
  7. Loosen from sides of pan while still warm.
  8. Cool on wire rack.

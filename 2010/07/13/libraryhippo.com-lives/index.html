2010-07-13 Miscellany LibraryHippo / Profligateness

For a while now, I've been dithering over getting LibraryHippo its own domain name. I hadn't, mostly because I'm a little lazy and cheap and afraid of the domain registration process. Yesterday at the Day Job, a co-worker (whose name just might be an anagram of Yen Waster), on learning that libraryhippo.com was available, plunked a $10 bill on my desk and insisted that I buy the domain. I called him foolish and tried to return the money, but he was adamant.
